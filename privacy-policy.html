2. Information We Collect

Because the core function of the Service is real-time tactical location sharing, we collect the following:

A. Personal Data

  • Geolocation Data: We collect precise, real-time location data (Latitude and Longitude). This is only collected when you actively enable Sender Mode or join a Convoy Room.
  • Background Location: To support the Emergency Intercept system and continuous Convoy tracking, the App collects location data even when the app is closed or not in use. This ensures your teammates can find you during an SOS event or a long-distance ride without keeping your screen on.

B. Non-Personal Data

  • Device Identifier (Shareable ID): A unique 9-character ID generated locally to link Senders and Receivers.
  • Status Telemetry: We collect the "Status" you select (e.g., HOME, CR, WAITING, EMERGENCY) to update your marker for other authorized users in your session.
  • Firebase UID & Advertising IDs: Used for secure database access and ad delivery via Google AdMob.

4. How Information is Shared (The Convoy Protocol)

  • Convoy Rooms: When you join a Room, your location and status are shared only with members of that specific room.
  • Emergency Signals: If you trigger an Emergency signal, your location is broadcast with high priority to your group, triggering a persistent vibration alarm on their devices.
  • Data Lifetime: Location data is temporary. Once a session is ended, all associated real-time coordinates are cleared from the active database.

5. User Control and Consent

  • Voluntary Sharing: Location sharing is 100% opt-in. No data is collected unless you manually grant permissions and start a session.
  • Background Permission: You will be prompted specifically for background location access. You can revoke this in device settings, though it will disable the "Emergency" and "Background Convoy" features.
  • Stopping Tracking: You can stop sharing at any time by pressing STOP SHARING or leaving the room. This immediately ceases data collection and deletes your active session history.

6. Security

With 9 years of professional development expertise, we utilize commercially acceptable encryption and Firebase security rules to protect your data. However, no electronic storage is 100% secure; we strive for maximum protection but cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service does not address anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover a child under 13 has provided data, we delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy for future HUD or telemetry enhancements. We will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
